## Story: Meta Cuts 8,000 Jobs, Freezes 6,000 Roles to Fund $115–145B AI Push
- **Date:** 2026-04-25
- **Source:** https://finance.biggo.com/news/202604260222_Meta-cuts-8000-jobs-for-AI
- **Outlet:** BigGo Finance / TechRepublic / The Next Web
- **Key facts:**
  - Meta to lay off ~8,000 employees (~10% of workforce); notifications begin May 20
  - 6,000 open job requisitions frozen
  - Severance: 16 weeks base pay + 2 weeks per year of service; health coverage up to 18 months
  - Meta reported record Q1 2026 revenue ($56.31B) and net income ($26.8B) — cuts not financial distress
  - 2026 AI capex forecast: $115–145 billion (double 2025 spend)
  - CEO Zuckerberg and CPO Janelle Gale cited AI infrastructure reallocation
  - Largest single layoff round since 2023 "year of efficiency"
- **Why it matters:** Proves even record-profit companies are converting payroll → AI infrastructure spend at unprecedented scale
- **Confidence:** high
- **Note:** Previous edition (Apr 17–24) referenced "Meta + Microsoft 20K+ combined" — some reporting overlap exists. The formal Meta announcement with specifics (8K figure, severance terms, May 20 notification date) is dated April 25–26.

## Story: Deloitte "State of AI in the Enterprise 2026" Report
- **Date:** ~2026-04-28 (released late April)
- **Source:** https://www.deloitte.com/us/en/what-we-do/capabilities/applied-artificial-intelligence/content/state-of-ai-in-the-enterprise.html
- **Outlet:** Deloitte
- **Key facts:**
  - Survey of 3,235 enterprise leaders
  - AI tool access grew from <40% to ~60% of workforce in one year
  - Only 25% of orgs moved ≥40% of AI experiments to production
  - 23% use agentic AI today; expected to reach 74% within 2 years
  - Only 21% have mature governance for autonomous AI agents
  - 84% have NOT redesigned jobs/workflows around AI capabilities
  - Only 34% say AI is "deeply transforming" their business
  - 58% using physical AI (robotics/IoT); expected 80% in 2 years
  - 83% see sovereign AI as strategically important
- **Why it matters:** Authoritative benchmark showing massive gap between AI ambition and operational reality — "pilot purgatory" remains the norm
- **Confidence:** high (primary source)
